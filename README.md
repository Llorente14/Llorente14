## Selected Work

### [LnFTI](https://github.com/Llorente14/LnFTI)

An AI-assisted lost-and-found Progressive Web App for the Faculty of Information Technology at Universitas Tarumanagara. LnFTI centralizes lost-item reports, found-item records, claims, verification, and handover tracking. YOLO and PaddleOCR support photo analysis while final decisions remain with users and campus officers.

`Next.js` · `TypeScript` · `Supabase` · `PostgreSQL` · `FastAPI` · `YOLO` · `PaddleOCR`

### [DAPUTE](https://github.com/Llorente14/Dapute)

An online ordering and management system for an Indonesian artisan-cookie MSME. The application covers product management, checkout, order tracking, financial reporting, administrative roles, and customer–staff confirmation workflows.

`Laravel` · `Livewire` · `Alpine.js` · `Tailwind CSS` · `Midtrans` · `Biteship`

[Live site](https://dapute.web.id)

### BerasMart

A team product concept for digitizing rice-cooperative inventory and supporting data-driven B2B matching. The proposed system connects supply and demand across surplus and deficit regions, provides fair-price recommendations, and explores traceability across the rice supply chain.

`Product Design` · `B2B Matching` · `Supply–Demand Data` · `Traceability`

### [Chirax](https://github.com/Llorente14/Chirax)

An early front-end project developed as an interactive personal media gallery with custom navigation, event sections, and organized image collections.

`HTML` · `CSS` · `JavaScript`
